### Key Components:
1. **Electric Motor**: Powers the hydraulic pump.
2. **Variable-Displacement Hydraulic Pump**: Adjusts fluid flow based on the control input.
3. **Control Stick**: The pilot's input device that initiates control surface movement.
4. **Flexible Hydraulic Line**: Transmits hydraulic fluid under pressure.
5. **Servovalve**: Regulates the flow of hydraulic fluid to the actuator.
6. **Hydraulic Cylinder**: Converts hydraulic pressure into mechanical motion to move the control surface.
7. **Viscous Damper**: Provides resistance to motion, enhancing stability and control feel.
