### Key Elements:
1. **Aerodynamic Center (AC)**: This is the point on an airfoil where the pitching moment remains constant with changes in angle of attack. It is crucial for stability analysis.
2. **Center of Gravity (CG)**: This is the point where the total weight of the aircraft is considered to act. The position of the CG affects the aircraft's balance and stability.
